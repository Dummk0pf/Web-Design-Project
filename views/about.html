Woz

It is known fact that consumer electronics can be better for all of us and for the environment. Unlike most products, ours are open for you to repair, upgrade and customize to your needs.

Our Inspiration

Our company is a tribute to the legend steve wozniak, Being literally and figuratively the backbone of what apple is today we figured he is much under appreciated for the skill he had in his time. Our first launching notebook is named after his invention the "Blue Box".

Our Motto

We aim to reduce the resources spent on making new notebooks by giving back the consumers their right to repair, a right which has become out of fashion, a right which will improve the livelihood of everyone
